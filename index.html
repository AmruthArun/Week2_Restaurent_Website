About Tasty Food

"Tasty Food, an oriental cuisine restaurant is one of Bangalore’s fastest-growing QSR (Quick service restaurant). The penchant for food and the passion of three motivated individuals reflected on the two decades of experience with quality control, service, and customer rapport in the food business. Tasty foods’s vision is to extend the authentic taste of the eastern kitchen to the taste buds, through every unique dish on our menu."

Our family of qualified chefs and service staff always strive to ensure that every dish served is served with love. With a boastful menu of more than 400 varieties ranging from soups, starters, main courses, and desserts mastered by our very skilled master chefs, we can assure that you will be spoilt for choice. Every order freshly prepared comes from our modern hygienic kitchens using the highest quality ingredients ensuring an authentic Chinese food experience.
